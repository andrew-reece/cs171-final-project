The Evolution of Social Networks:

Visual reports from the MIT Social Evolution experiment

This display shows the evolution of communication networks over time.
Edge weights represent call/sms activity between individuals.
Frequency counts are updated in two-week intervals, for the ~18 month duration of the study.
